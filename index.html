About Us:
Geek Gazette is the official technical magazine of IIT Roorkee with content specifically curated for exuberant, technogeek, and apprized inhabitants of IIT Roorkee and beyond.
We are a coterie of opinionated technophiles and philomathic nerds who work throughout the semester to present our semesterly print magazines and oodles of online content, compiling of enthralling articles, comics, engaging web series, tech reviews, newfangled tech-trends, art recommendations, pertinent interviews, and any other domain to every other domain we are geeky about.
Established in 2009, this magazine has grown by leaps and bounds, upholding the motto – “from the geeks, by the geeks, for the geeks”, since its inception.
